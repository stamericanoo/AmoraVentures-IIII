Sulfuric Acid Plant Design – 1000 Ton/Day Capacity

Contact Process – Double Absorption – Technical & Economic Evaluation

1.0 Project Background

Sulfuric acid is one of the most important industrial chemicals globally used in fertilizer, petroleum refining, mineral processing and chemical synthesis.

2.0 Design Basis & Assumptions

  • Plant Capacity: 1000 ton/day
  • Operating Days: 330 days/year
  • Overall Efficiency: 98%
  • Sulfur Purity: >99.5%
  • Exchange Rate: 1 USD = 16,000 IDR

3.0 Process Selection Justification

  • High conversion efficiency
  • Low emission
  • Economic feasibility
  • Industrial reliability

4.0 Stoichiometric Derivation

S + O₂ → SO₂ 2SO₂ + O₂ → 2SO₃ SO₃ + H₂O → H₂SO₄

Theoretical sulfur requirement: 0.3265 ton S per ton H₂SO₄.

For 1000 TPD → 326.5 ton/day theoretical.

Adjusted input: 181 ton/day.

5.0 Sulfur Burning Furnace

S (l) + O₂ (g) → SO₂ (g) ΔH = -296 kJ/mol

Energy release ≈ 1.66 × 10⁹ kJ/day.

6.0 Gas Cleaning & Drying

  • Electrostatic Precipitator
  • Gas Cooler
  • Drying Tower

Moisture below 0.1% before converter.

2SO₂ + O₂ ⇌ 2SO₃
Temp420–450°C
